Crunchy Quesadilla Recipe!
Delicious Stacks of Cheese Quesadillas

This recipe makes for the easiest, fastest, and most delicious quesadilla. With only 3 required ingredients, its a simple breakfast, lunch or even dinner that you can make without leaving the comfort of your home and without spending much.This quesadilla recipe is also a one pot-recipe so cleaning will not be a hassle.

Ingredients:

  • Large flour tortillas
  • Grated cheese - either mild or sharp cheddar, or Monterey Jack
  • Olive oil or butter

Optional:

  • Bite size chicken pieces
  • Avocado
  • Lettuce
  • Mushroom

Steps:

  1. Place a tortilla on a hot griddle
  2. Let it brown
  3. Flip it over
  4. Fill with cheese and toppings of your choice. When fillings are warmed through and the tortilla is sufficiently browned
  5. Fold the tortilla over
  6. Remove it from the griddle and enjoy
