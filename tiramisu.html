Classic Tiramisu Recipe

Creamy dessert of espresso-soaked ladyfingers, rich mascarpone, and sweetend whipped cream; Flavors that melt in your mouth!

This delicious and unbelievably easy Tiramisu recipe is made with coffee soaked lady fingers, sweet and creamy mascarpone, and cocoa powder dusted on top. It requires no baking and can be made in advance!

Ingredients:

  • 1 Cup espresso
  • 2 tablespoons of rum or cognac
  • 2 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 24 Ladyfingers
  • 1 to 2 ounces of bittersweet chocolate

For The Cream:

  • 4 large egg yolks
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup of Mascarpone

Steps:

  1. Whip together egg yolks and 1/4 cup/50 grams sugar until very pale yellow and about tripled in volume
  2. Transfer mixture to a large bowl, wiping out the medium bowl used to whip the yolks and set aside
  3. In the medium bowl, whip cream and remaining 1/4 cup/50 grams sugar until it creates soft-medium peaks
  4. Add mascarpone and continue to whip until it creates a soft and fold the mascapone into the egg yolks until combined
  5. Combine espresso and rum in a shallow bowl and set aside
  6. dust the bottom of a 2-quart baking dish with 1 tablespoon of cocoa powder
  7. Dip each ladyfinger into the espresso and place them rounded side up at the bottom of the baking dish. Repeat until bottom of the baking dish is full
  8. Spread half of the Mascarpone micture onto the ladyfingers in one even layer
  9. Repeat with remaining ladyfingers and Mascarpone mixture
  10. Dust top layer with remaining tablespoon of cocoa powder
  11. Cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 4 hours before serving
