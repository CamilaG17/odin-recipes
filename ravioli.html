Creamy Ravioli Recipe!
Creamy and savory at home Italian Ravioli

This mouthwatering ravioli recipe has enough of a restaurant quality that it will have people questioning if you really did make it from home, and under 20 minutes! his easy pasta dish is made using store-bought refrigerated ravioli then smothered in a rich garlic parmesan cream sauce with fresh baby spinach and sun-dried tomatoes.

Ingredients:
  • Store bought Ravioli
  • Sun-dried Tomatoes
  • Heavy Cream
  • Parmesan
  • White Wine
  • Unsalted Butter
  • Minced Garlic
  • Olive Oil
  • Italian Seasoning
Steps:
  1. Boil your ravioli according to the package instructions and drain when finished
  2. Add salt and 1 tablespoon of olive oil to your pasta water so that it does not stick
  3. In a large saute pan, add the olive oil then melt the butter into the oil over medium heat
  4. Then add the Italian seasoning to the pan and swirl to combine
  5. Add the minced garlic to the pan and cook for about 1 minute
  6. Add the wine to the pan and simmer for about 2 to 3 minutes
  7. Stir in the heavy cream and bring just to a boil, then keep at a simmer for 6 to 8 minutes, whisking occasionally
  8. Reduce the heat to low then add the finely grated parmesan cheese to the pan and quickly whisk to combine
  9. stir in the spinach and sundried tomatoes
  10. Add the cooked ravioli to the sauce and gently stir and spoon the sauce over the ravioli to coat them. Serve hot and enjoy!
